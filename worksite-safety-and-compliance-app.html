The StaffApps Safety Pack is a straightforward app designed to get your safety compliance sorted, fast. We take the paperwork you have to do and make it something you want to do—because it's quick, simple, and makes your business better.

Everything You Need, Nothing You Don't

Our Safety Pack combines the three most critical safety forms into one easy-to-use app for your whole crew.

Legally Compliant SWMS

Create, review, and get the entire crew to sign off on a compliant Safe Work Method Statement right on a phone or tablet. We even pre-load your most common high-risk tasks to turn a 20-minute job into a 2-minute check.

Daily Site Risk Assessments (JSAs)

Start each day with a quick, smart checklist to identify and control site-specific risks. Attach photos of hazards, add notes, and create a time-stamped digital record that proves your commitment to safety.

Vehicle & Plant Pre-Start Checklists

Your crew can run through a 60-second check on their ute, truck, or excavator each morning. If they spot a fault, you get an instant email alert, so you can schedule a repair before it causes costly downtime.
