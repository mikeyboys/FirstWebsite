Government Security Assessments

We conduct comprehensive security clearance assessments on behalf of the Australian Government. Our highly experienced and qualified officers handle all clearance levels with the utmost professionalism and confidentiality.

  • Baseline Vetting (BL)
  • Negative Vetting Level 1 (NV1)
  • Negative Vetting Level 2 (NV2)
  • Positive Vetting (PV)

DISP Membership Consultation

Navigating the Defence Industry Security Program (DISP) can be complex. We provide expert guidance to help your business meet the security requirements for DISP membership, enabling you to partner with the Australian Defence industry.

  • Governance & Security Risk Management
  • Personnel & Physical Security
  • Information & Cyber Security
  • Achieve and maintain DISP accreditation

About Us

SIRRAS Consultants is a premier security risk management company with extensive expertise in government security vetting and private sector employment screening. Our team is comprised of highly qualified professionals from diverse backgrounds, including intelligence, security, law enforcement, and military counter-intelligence.

We pride ourselves on our exceptional reputation and an unparalleled level of support for our clients. As part of the working group that developed the Australian Standard AS4811-2006 for 'Employment Screening', we are leaders in establishing best practices within the industry.

  • Unmatched Expertise: Decades of combined experience in government and defence security.
  • Professional & Timely: Efficient and thorough processes to meet your deadlines.
  • Australia-Wide: Representatives in all Australian states and territories to support your needs.
